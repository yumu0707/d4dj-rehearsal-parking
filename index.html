Bonus Percentage Reference Tables

  • No Match: neither Style nor Character match. Give 0% bonus regardless of rarity and extra training status.
  • Single Match: either Style OR Character match.
  • Both Match: both Style AND Character match.
  • All single match [et0] cards give 20% bonus regardless of rarity.
  • All both match [et0] cards give 50% bonus regardless of rarity.
  • You can use 1★ and 2★ cards for the above two cases.
